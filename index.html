About Our Community

A Premium Residential Community in Kasba

Signature 18 Association of Apartment Owners is the governing body of the 69-apartment residential tower located at 8, Bosepukur Road, Kasba, Kolkata.

The Association oversees the day-to-day operations, maintenance, community initiatives, and resident welfare, ensuring that the building functions seamlessly.

Committed to maintaining the highest standards of living, we strive to create a community where every family enjoys comfort, safety, and a true sense of belonging.

Our Vision

Our vision is to nurture Signature 18 as a model residential community — one that reflects thoughtful management, mutual respect among residents, and pride in our shared home.

Through responsible governance and active community participation, we aim to preserve the quality, value, and harmony of our residence for years to come.
